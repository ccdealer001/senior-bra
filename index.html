About Warren Buffett

Legendary Investor & Philanthropist

Warren Buffett is one of the world's most successful investors and business magnates, serving as the chairman and CEO of Berkshire Hathaway. Born in 1930 in Omaha, Nebraska, Buffett developed an interest in business and investing at a young age.

Beyond his extraordinary business achievements, Buffett is known for his commitment to philanthropy. In 2006, he pledged to donate over 99% of his wealth to charitable causes, primarily through the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation and through foundations established by his family members.

"If you're in the luckiest 1% of humanity, you owe it to the rest of humanity to think about the other 99%."

Through the Warren Foundation, we continue his legacy of thoughtful, effective giving that focuses on solving some of society's most pressing challenges while creating lasting, positive change for future generations.

Grant Focus Areas

Global Health

Supporting innovative approaches to healthcare access, disease prevention, and medical research that improves health outcomes for underserved populations worldwide.

Education

Investing in educational excellence, from early childhood development to higher education, with special focus on expanding access to quality education for disadvantaged communities.

Economic Opportunity

Creating pathways out of poverty through financial inclusion, entrepreneurship support, workforce development, and building more equitable economic systems.

Climate Change & Environment

Addressing our planet's most pressing environmental challenges through conservation efforts, clean energy innovation, and sustainable development practices.

Executive MBA Scholarship Program

Supporting Future Business Leaders

Our foundation proudly supports future business leaders through our Executive MBA Scholarship Program. Each year, we award scholarships to outstanding candidates who demonstrate exceptional potential in investment strategies and business innovation.

The Warren Buffett Investment Challenge is our flagship educational initiative, providing students with hands-on experience in portfolio management and investment analysis while competing for scholarship funds.

Since 2015, we have awarded over $250,000 in educational scholarships to 45 promising business students.
